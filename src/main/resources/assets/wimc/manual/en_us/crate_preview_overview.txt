Engineer's Crate Preview
Look inside before unpacking
<&crate_icons>This client-side inspector adds a compact inventory preview to supported crate item tooltips.
Hover a sealed crate stack to see its stored items, stack counts, and empty slots when the setting is enabled.<br>
It does not open the crate, request server data, or generate loot-table contents on the client.<np>
Use this page as a quick check at a workbench, warehouse wall, or storage shelf before placing crates back down.
For crate support and recipes, see <link;wimc:supported_crates;supported crates>. For display options, see <link;wimc:preview_settings;preview settings>.
