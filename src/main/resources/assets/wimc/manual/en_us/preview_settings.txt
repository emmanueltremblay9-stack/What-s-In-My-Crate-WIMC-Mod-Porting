Preview Settings
Make the inspector fit your workshop
<&settings_icon>The preview is controlled by the WIMC client config.
By default it is enabled, does not require Shift, shows empty slots, and renders up to six rows.<br>
The Immersive Engineering crate detector and this mod's opt-in crate detector can be toggled separately.<np>
Use `requireShiftForPreview` if large tooltips get in your way.
Use `maxPreviewRows` to cap tall previews, and `showEmptySlots` to choose between a full crate-shaped grid or only occupied slots.<br>
When the inventory is larger than the visible area, the tooltip reports how many slots are hidden.<np>
Loot-table-only crates are shown as unresolved because the client cannot safely know their future contents.
Malformed item data is ignored defensively so a bad stack should not break the manual, the tooltip, or dedicated server startup.
